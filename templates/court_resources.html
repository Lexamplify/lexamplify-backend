🔏 e-Notarisation

Legally valid digital notarisation under the IT Act, 2000 — recognised across India.

ℹ️ e-Notarisation using Aadhaar-based eSign is legally valid under the Information Technology Act, 2000 (Section 5) and the IT (Amendment) Act, 2008. Electronically signed and notarised documents carry the same legal force as paper-notarised documents in civil matters.
